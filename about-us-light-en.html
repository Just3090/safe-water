   Our Vision

Our mission is to provide communities with access to quality drinking water. We aim to transform the way communities access and manage water, using innovative and sustainable technologies to ensure a healthy and prosperous future for all.

   Who we are

Our team of engineers, scientists, and activists is dedicated to enhancing global water quality. We are developing a prototype waterproof robot that utilizes tidal energy to move and filter water, ensuring its safety for human consumption and benefit to the environment.


   Ethic of our Team

We are guided by the principles of transparency, sustainability and social responsibility. We believe in the importance of working with society and work closely with local communities to understand their needs and develop solutions that truly make a difference. We are committed to human health and environmental stewardship, ensuring that our actions today do not compromise the well-being of future generations.

   SDG 6

We are aligned with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 6, which aims to ensure the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. Through our technological innovations and community efforts, we are contributing to the goal of universal access to safe and affordable drinking water.
