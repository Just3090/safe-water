 Plan: Clean and Safe Water

The project focuses on improving the quality of drinking water through the use of filters and advanced chemical technologies to ensure that all people have access to clean and safe water.

In some parts of the world, lack of access to safe drinking water is a critical problem affecting millions of people. Poor water quality directly affects human health, causing gastrointestinal diseases such as diarrhea and dehydration, especially in underserved communities. Solving this problem is essential to ensuring access to quality drinking water and preventing disease, thereby contributing to human well-being and environmental sustainability.
