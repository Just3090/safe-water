 Awareness campaign


 Campaign Objective

Our objective is to guarantee that all individuals have access to potable water of the highest quality. Through this initiative, we aim to educate and mobilize the community to address the issue of poor water quality and promote sustainable solutions.


 Problem Identified

The quality of water is a critical issue affecting human health and ecosystems. Pollution of water sources can result in a range of adverse effects, including the emergence of gastrointestinal diseases, disruption to aquatic biodiversity, and the limitation of access to safe drinking water.


 Suggested Solution

Our team has developed an innovative prototype: an aquatic robot that uses tidal energy to move and filter water. This robot incorporates filters and controlled chemicals to purify the water, eliminating contaminants and improving its quality.
